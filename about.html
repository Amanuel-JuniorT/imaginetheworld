WHO WE ARE


The Leading Scholarship Fund Since 2000


Founded in 2000, Imagine The World was established under the foundation of giving. We give everyone the chance to donate to a good cause, and believe that a little monetary contribution can go a really long way in improving the lives of others. Our grants spread across a variety of different fields, and have been the driving force behind the success of those in need.

OUR MISSION


Fostering the Lives of Others


At Imagine The World , we believe that receiving the proper support and guidance can transform an already bright individual into so much more. We are here to be that support system, and help our recipients reach their fullest potential and bring all of their dreams to life. All of our recipients are intelligent, bold, and undeniably personable, making our work at Imagine The World incredibly meaningful and worthwhile.

Over the years, our contributions have earned us the reputation of being the best Scholarship Fund in the greater San Francisco area — and we couldn’t be any happier to pursue what it is we love the most.
